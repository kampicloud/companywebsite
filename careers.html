Kampi Cloud Screening Process

1

Initial Screening

When you click "Apply Now" and schedule an interview through our Calendly link, we'll conduct the first interview to assess your qualifications and suitability for the role.
2

Partner Interview

In the second interview, you'll meet with all Kampi Cloud partners. This ensures alignment with our values, culture, and expectations. Passing this stage indicates you're a good fit for our organization.
3

Client Interview

Successful candidates from the partner interview move on to the third interview with the potential client. Here, the client evaluates your compatibility with their specific requirements.
4

Onboarding with the Client

After successfully passing the client interview, we focus on integrating the VA seamlessly into the client's daily tasks and software systems, ensuring a smooth transition and productive collaboration.
