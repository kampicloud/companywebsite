Application Process

Whether you need a proof of residency, a clearance, or any other barangay certificate, you can complete the process comfortably from your home. Follow these simple steps to get started

1

Register and Sign In

First, create an account on our barangay’s online portal. If you already have an account, simply sign in with your credentials. This account will allow you to access and request various services and documents.
2

Apply for the Certificate You Need

Once signed in, choose the specific certificate you require and fill out the necessary information in the application form. Ensure that all details are correct to avoid any delays.
3

We Process Your Application

After you submit your application, we will begin processing it immediately. We will verify the information and prepare your certificate. You will receive updates and notifications about the status of your application through the system.
4

Specification

Once processed, your certificate will be available for download directly from the online portal. Alternatively, you can choose to pick it up at the barangay office if required. We ensure a quick and secure delivery of all documents.
